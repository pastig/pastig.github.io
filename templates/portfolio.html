Rocket Booster

I made this little helicopter-type game called "Rocket Booster" in Unity, using the Unity Game Engine with C# scripting.

The player can steer the rocket using keyboard inputs.

Finally, I used to level manager so the player could progress to other levels.

Kalaha

I recreated good old Kalaha game in Python.

The game can be played by two players, or simulated by two computer players.

Here, I'm showing the results of simulating a thousand Kalaha games.


By these simulations I found that Player 1 (the starting player) has a significant advantage, why I finally found that putting just one more stone in Player 2's 4th pocket minimized the advantage to ~ 1 %. I'm printing all board states to screen in this simulation, but could turn that printing off to optimize the speed of the simulation. I created a bit of ASCII-art to visualize the board states.
